About Me

Jessica Del Grande

Front-End Developer

I'm a front-end developer with extensive experience managing data-driven digital and direct marketing projects in organizations of all sizes, from startups to international corporations. I'm an excellent communicator, a fearless question asker, and a lifelong learner.

My background in advertising and production has refined my eye for layout and my passion for clean, modular design that flows seamlessly between devices of all sizes. I'm currently coding in Sublime and using HTML5, CSS3, Sass/SCSS, JavaScript, ES6, jQuery and other JavaScript libraries, React, WordPress, PHP, Bootstrap, Git, GitHub, Gulp, Firebase, and APIs (AJAX), building on my fascination with data and my deep respect for JavaScript.

When I'm not coding, I can be found watching documentaries and foreign films, volunteering in Toronto's tech community, concocting herbal remedies, and trying to beat my grandpa at crokinole.
